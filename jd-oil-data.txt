Position Summary
The Update Processor plays a critical role in maintaining the accuracy and integrity of our digital title plant by entering and updating county courthouse records. This position requires strong computer proficiency, exceptional attention to detail, and a consistent, reliable work ethic. The ideal candidate is highly organized, focused, and committed to producing precise work in a fast-paced environment.

Key Responsibilities
Enter county courthouse data into the digital title plant with a high level of accuracy

Review public records, filings, and legal documents for completeness and correctness

Index, update, and maintain property and title information according to company standards

Verify data integrity and identify discrepancies or missing information

Maintain productivity goals while ensuring quality control standards are met

Follow established procedures for document handling, naming conventions, and indexing

Communicate with supervisors regarding issues, questions, or irregular records

Maintain confidentiality of sensitive information at all times

Required Skills & Qualifications
Excellent attention to detail with strong accuracy in data entry

Strong work ethic and ability to stay focused on repetitive, detail-driven tasks

Advanced computer proficiency, including navigating multiple systems and databases

Ability to learn new software platforms and digital title plant systems quickly

Strong organizational and time-management skills

Dependable, self-motivated, and able to work independently

Preferred Experience
Experience working with county courthouse records, title plants, or public record databases

Background in title insurance, oil & gas land services, real estate, or legal document processing

Prior data entry or indexing experience in a high-volume environment
