Responsibilities:

Lead the analysis of business processes and workflows to identify areas for improvement and optimization.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to gather, document, and validate business requirements.
Conduct gap analyses to identify discrepancies between current and desired outcomes.
Facilitate Agile Scrum ceremonies, including sprint planning, review, and retrospectives.
Review and analyze documentation to ensure alignment with business objectives.
Work closely with call center teams to enhance customer service processes and systems.
Communicate findings and recommendations to stakeholders in a clear and concise manner.
Support the development and implementation of solutions that align with organizational goals.
Monitor project progress and ensure deliverables meet quality standards.
Provide ongoing support and training to teams on new processes and tools.
Requirements
Proven experience as a Business Analyst, preferably in a senior role.
Strong understanding of business analysis methodologies and tools.
Proficiency in conducting gap analyses and identifying process improvement opportunities.
Experience working in Agile Scrum environments, including facilitating related ceremonies.
Familiarity with call center operations and customer service best practices.
Excellent documentation and communication skills.
Ability to collaborate effectively with diverse teams and stakeholders.
Detail-oriented with strong problem-solving and analytical abilities.
